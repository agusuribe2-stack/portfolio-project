# LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy Research for B2B SaaS

## Project Overview

This repository contains a structured research project developed as part of a technical hiring assignment focused on LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS companies.

Rather than collecting generic marketing resources, the objective was to build a high-quality research library based on practitioners who actively execute, teach, and publish modern organic growth strategies.

The repository prioritizes evidence quality, source traceability, and reusable strategic knowledge over research volume.

---

## Research Objective

The project aims to answer a central question:

> What recurring principles define successful LinkedIn organic content strategies for modern B2B SaaS companies?

To answer this question, the research focuses on identifying experienced practitioners, documenting their publicly available content, and extracting transferable strategic knowledge that can later be synthesized into an evidence-based playbook.

---

## Research Methodology

The research followed four sequential phases.

### 1. Expert Selection

Ten recognized B2B SaaS practitioners were selected based on:

- Active LinkedIn publishing
- Practical implementation experience
- Public educational content
- Strong reputation within the SaaS ecosystem
- Relevance to organic growth and LinkedIn strategy

Preference was given to operators rather than general marketing influencers.

### 2. Source Collection

For each expert, approximately five high-signal public sources were collected.

Whenever LinkedIn posts were not publicly accessible, official substitute sources were used, including:

- Company blogs
- Newsletters
- Podcast episode pages
- Public articles
- Research publications
- Author essays

Every source remains fully traceable through its original URL.

### 3. Research Documentation

Each source was transformed into a structured research note containing:

- Source information
- Public content summary
- Key ideas
- Frameworks or methodologies
- Practical takeaways
- Strategic relevance
- Verification notes

The goal was knowledge preservation rather than simple bookmarking.

### 4. Pattern Synthesis

The individual research notes serve as the foundation for identifying recurring themes across experts.

Cross-source synthesis is documented separately in `research/findings.md`.

## Research Principles

The project follows several guiding principles throughout the repository.

### Evidence over assumptions

Only publicly verifiable information is included.

### Traceability

Every insight can be traced back to its original source.

### Knowledge preservation

Research notes capture transferable strategic knowledge instead of simply archiving links.

### Consistency

Terminology and documentation structure remain consistent across all research notes.

### Quality over quantity

The objective is not to maximize the number of collected sources but to maximize their practical value.

---

## Research Limitations

Several LinkedIn posts were not publicly accessible without authentication.

Whenever this occurred, the research relied on official public substitutes such as newsletters, podcast pages, or company publications.

These substitutions are explicitly documented within each research note.

No unsupported assumptions or fabricated conclusions were added.
