## Public Content Summary

This source is a Positioning podcast episode summary about differentiated value. The public summary says differentiated value is the most important and misunderstood part of positioning. The problem addressed is that companies may talk about features, benefits, or claims without making clear why their value is meaningfully different from alternatives.

The source teaches that differentiation is not optional in positioning. For B2B SaaS LinkedIn strategy, this matters because content that does not communicate differentiated value often blends into category noise. The source supports the idea that posts should connect product capabilities or beliefs to a distinct value claim.
