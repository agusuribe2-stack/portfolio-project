## Public Content Summary

This source is a Positioning podcast episode summary about preparing for a positioning exercise. The public summary says positioning fails when teams skip preparation, ignore alignment, or try to serve every customer. The problem addressed is internal: companies can weaken positioning before the work begins by failing to prepare or by avoiding hard choices.

The source teaches that focus and alignment matter. For LinkedIn strategy, this is relevant because public content often exposes internal confusion. If a company tries to speak to every customer or every use case, its posts may become broad and forgettable. The source supports preparation as a prerequisite for stronger positioning and stronger public messaging.
