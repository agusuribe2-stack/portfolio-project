## Public Content Summary

This source is a SparkToro author archive listing about measuring lift-based marketing investments. The listing frames lift-based experimentation as an alternative when attribution is unreliable. The problem addressed is measurement: many marketing activities, especially organic and brand-building work, influence buyers without producing clean click or conversion attribution.

The source supports the idea that marketers need measurement approaches beyond direct attribution. For LinkedIn strategy, this is central because organic LinkedIn may shape awareness, trust, and preference before a buyer visits a website or fills out a form. The accessible listing does not provide the exact metrics or experiment design, but it verifies the measurement concept.
