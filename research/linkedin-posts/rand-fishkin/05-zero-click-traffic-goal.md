## Public Content Summary

This source is a SparkToro author archive listing for an article about traffic as a poor goal in a zero-click world. The listing says many marketers still use traffic as a primary goal or KPI despite reduced referral traffic. The problem addressed is a mismatch between user behavior and marketing measurement: platforms increasingly keep users on-platform, while marketers continue to optimize for visits.

The source teaches that traffic is not always the best proxy for marketing value. For LinkedIn strategy, this is directly relevant because many valuable interactions happen inside the feed without a click. The accessible source is listing-level, so it does not provide the full argument, but it supports the central zero-click measurement concern.
